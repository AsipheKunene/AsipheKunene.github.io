The Red Rumour

Logo Design | Social Media | Branding

For The Red Rumour, a trendy speakeasy-style bar, I crafted a distinct and memorable brand identity that echoes its unique ambiance. The project encompassed logo design, comprehensive branding, and an engaging social media strategy. The logo captures the bar's vintage allure with modern flair, using bold typography and subtle nods to classic speakeasy aesthetics.

Branding elements, including menus, signage, and promotional materials, maintain a cohesive and sophisticated look. The social media design effectively showcases the bar's vibrant atmosphere and special events, and establishing The Red Rumour as a go-to destination for nightlife enthusiasts.
