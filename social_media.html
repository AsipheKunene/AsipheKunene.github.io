Social Media

Social Media design from multiple projects

Irene Country Lodge, Autograph Collection®

For Irene Country Lodge, Autograph Collection®, I designed a series of social media visuals that capture the essence of its authentic farm experience. The content features stunning imagery of the lodge’s picturesque surroundings and luxurious accommodations, enhanced by rustic, elegant design elements that evoke a sense of tranquility and charm.


Kuveza Online Gallery

The design emphasises the gallery’s commitment to artisanal craftsmanship and environmental responsibility. Each post is curated to highlight the unique textures and details of the sculptures, complemented by engaging storytelling that connects the art to its creators and the natural world. This approach not only showcases the gallery’s offerings but also builds a narrative that resonates with art enthusiasts and eco-conscious buyers.


Absolute Credit Control Services

For Absolute Credit Control Services, I undertook a comprehensive redesign of their social media presence to better reflect their expertise and professionalism in credit management. The new design features a sleek and modern aesthetic, incorporating clean lines and a consistent colour scheme that enhances brand recognition.
